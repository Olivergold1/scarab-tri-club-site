Meet the Team

Friendly, qualified coaches who run our sessions.

Andy Preece

Head swim coach — ASA Level 2. Leads structured pool sessions with a focus on technique, drills and endurance.

Simon Clapham

Simon founded the club when there was only one other triathlon club in South Manchester. He has been with SCARAB ever since and serves as our lead coach. Qualifications: Level 3 Swim Coaching, Level 2 Triathlon Coaching.

Jason Stott

Jason is heavily involved with the running side of SCARAB through our sister club, Wilmslow Striders. He supports members of all abilities and helps coordinate run sessions.
