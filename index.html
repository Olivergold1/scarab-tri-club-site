What do I need for my first session? +
Bring swimwear for pool sessions (goggles recommended), a road or hybrid bike for rides, and trainers for run sessions. A positive attitude! Pay-as-you-go participants can pay on arrival.
Do I need to be fast to join? +
Not at all ‚Äî we welcome all abilities. Sessions are structured to accommodate different experience levels.
Where do you meet for rides? +
Meet at Wilmslow town centre or specified cafe points. Check the WhatsApp/Strava club for weekly ride meet locations.
