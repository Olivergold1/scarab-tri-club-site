Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about joining, training and taking part in club activities.

Do I need to be fit to join?
Not at all — we welcome all abilities, from beginners to experienced triathletes. Sessions are structured so you can train at your own pace.
Can I try a session before joining?
Yes — you’re welcome to try a swim, bike or run session before committing to membership.
What equipment do I need?
For swimming: goggles, swimwear and a pull buoy. For cycling: a roadworthy bike and helmet. For running: suitable trainers. No specialist kit is required to start.
Do you offer coached sessions?
Yes — we offer coached swim sessions and structured run sessions through Wilmslow Striders.
How do I join the club?
Simply complete the form on our Membership page and we’ll get back to you with next steps.
