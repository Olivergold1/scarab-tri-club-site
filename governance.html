Governance

Our club is run by volunteers and follows clear policies to ensure a safe, inclusive and well‑managed environment for all members.

Club Policies

We follow guidance from British Triathlon and maintain policies covering:

  • Code of Conduct
  • Health & Safety
  • Data Protection (GDPR)
  • Equality, Diversity & Inclusion

Safeguarding

The safety and wellbeing of all members is our top priority. We follow British Triathlon safeguarding standards and ensure:

  • All coaches are DBS checked
  • Clear reporting procedures
  • Trained Welfare Officers

Annual General Meetings

Scarab Tri Club holds its Annual General Meeting (AGM) around the end of March each year. Members are invited to review the club’s progress, finances, and future plans.
